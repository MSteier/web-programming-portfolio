Gaboon Viper

Gaboon viper (Bitis gabonica), is a highly venomous snake from rainforests and savannas of sub-Saharan Africa. Gaboon vipers have the longest fangs of all venomous snakes it has the longest fangs of any venomous snake – up to 2 inches (5 cm) in length. they have a lightning fast strike and a a venom that is both cytotoxic meaning it attacks cells and cardiotoxic which means it effects heart muscles. |Return to map|

Quick Facts

  • Largest fangs of all venomous species
  • 5 feet long
  • Eats birds and rodents
