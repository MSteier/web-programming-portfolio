Sonoran Coral

Sonoran Coralsnake (Micruroides euryxanthus) is one of three variatations of coral snakes found in the United States which include the Sonoaran Coral Snake the Eastern Coral Snake and the Eastern Coral Snake. As adults they can reach about 2 feet in length. Although highly venomous these snakes are generally not considered agressive. Coral snakes enjoy a diverse diet which includes, rodents, lizards and smaller snakes. |Return to map|
